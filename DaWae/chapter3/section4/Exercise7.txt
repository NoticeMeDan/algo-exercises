3.4.7

The change mix up the bits sufficiently well that a nonprime M can be used.

Example where a is an arbitrary fixed prime:
a = 7
k = 1, 2, 3, 4
M = 6

(7 * 1) % 6 = 1
(7 * 2) % 6 = 2
(7 * 3) % 6 = 3
(7 * 4) % 6 = 4
(7 * 5) % 6 = 5
(7 * 6) % 6 = 0

Example where a is not an arbitrary fixed prime:
a = 8
k = 1, 2, 3, 4
M = 6

(8 * 1) % 6 = 2
(8 * 2) % 6 = 4
(8 * 3) % 6 = 0
(8 * 4) % 6 = 2
(8 * 5) % 6 = 4
(8 * 6) % 6 = 0
